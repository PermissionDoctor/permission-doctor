# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Permission Doctor is a Discord bot that helps server owners audit, diagnose, repair, and manage Discord permissions.

## Data Collection

Permission Doctor does not permanently collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

The bot may temporarily access Discord-provided information required to execute commands, including:

- Server IDs
- Channel IDs
- Role information
- Member information
- Permission settings

## Data Storage

Permission Doctor does not maintain a database of user data or message content.

## Data Sharing

Permission Doctor does not sell, rent, or share data with third parties.

## Third-Party Services

Permission Doctor relies on Discord and is subject to Discord's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

## Security

Reasonable efforts are made to keep the bot secure and reliable.

## Changes

This policy may be updated periodically.

## Contact

For support or privacy questions, use the official Permission Doctor support server.
